How does it work?


Incredible Beef is moving forward with ground breaking research in bacteria, studying specific bacterial species uniquely found in the human navel.

This page will assist this research in displaying the data for each of the bacterial cultures found in the participant's navel. A dropdown menu lists the ID numbers of all the volunteers. When a volunteer ID is chosen from the dropdown menu, that participant's information will be displayed.

Demographic Information: The participant demographics information, such as location, sex, and age will be displayed.

Bar Chart: This chart displays the Operational Taxonomic Unit (OTU), number of the bacteria culture(s) found.

Gauge: The gauge chart displays the weekly washing frequency.

Bubble Chart: The most dynamic chart of all visualizes the overall data along with indices of size. This includes OTU-ids, sample values and OTU labels defining all of the bacteria types found per participant.
